Seminar Series on Engineering Neuroscience and Health


There are multiple ways to keep up-to-date with the Engineering Neuroscience and Health Seminar Series.

On the web:
http://www-clmc.usc.edu/~heiko/ENH/

On Google Calendar:
Search for "ENH Seminar Series at USC" in public google calendars. You may add it to your calendar.

On iCal and iPhone:
In the menu bar, select "Calendar ... Subscribe" and add
http://www.google.com/calendar/ical/enhatusc%40gmail.com/public/basic.ics
to the URL. This calendar can then easily be added to your iPhone or iPod Touch the next time you synchronize with your computer. Each time you synchronize, your ENH Seminar Series calendar will be brought up-to-date.
